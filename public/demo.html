Picture a face on your website that greets every visitor — in your voice, on your brand, ready to answer the question your FAQ never quite covers. It looks back at you. It listens. It speaks. And it remembers what your business is supposed to sound like.

A person-shaped presence — trained on your content, voiced in your tone, online before your team has had their coffee. Visitors who would have bounced stay, talk, and tell you exactly what they're looking for. Keep scrolling. The rest of this page is what that actually looks like.

Features & Functionality

Every Divigner avatar ships with the same conversational core, then dials in the policies, integrations, and tone for the brand it represents.

i

Voice & text in one

Visitors can speak or type — the avatar replies in either modality, with natural lip-sync and a brand-matched voice profile.

ii

Trained on your content

We pull from your website, knowledge base, and approved documents. The avatar answers from your sources, not the open internet.

iii

Smart routing

Questions outside the avatar's scope — billing, clinical, escalation — get routed back to a real human on your team, with full transcript context.

iv

Compliance built-in

Educational disclaimers, PHI guardrails, and refusal policies are wired in by default and reviewed for every regulated vertical.

v

Drop-in embed

One snippet on your site, hosted on your subdomain, with no plugin or rebuild required. Updates push instantly from the Divigner Portal.

vi

Analytics & transcripts

Every conversation is logged, summarized, and tagged by intent — so you can see what visitors actually ask, week over week.

And here's what it does for your business
01

Talk to your visitors before they bounce

Most of your traffic leaves without ever raising their hand. An avatar opens a low-friction door — voice or text, on their schedule — and captures the intent that contact forms miss.

02

Speak the visitor's language, every time

The same avatar handles routine questions across multiple languages with consistent brand voice, freeing your team from repetitive replies.

03

Educate without overcommitting

For regulated practices (medical, legal, financial), the avatar answers educational questions with the right disclaimers and routes personal questions to a human — fast.

04

A face the brand can scale

One avatar can be on the homepage, in the resource center, embedded in a campaign landing page, and inside a partner microsite — all consistent, all trained on the same source of truth.

05

Real measurable lift

Avatar sessions are tagged, summarized, and routed back into your CRM — so you can see which conversations turned into appointments, applications, or qualified inbound leads.
