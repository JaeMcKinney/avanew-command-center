What are Interactive Avatars?

Picture a face inside your CME activity, or right on your practice's website, that greets every learner and patient, answers in your team's voice, and never goes off-label. It looks back at you. It listens. It speaks. And it answers only from the approved content you gave it.

Not a module→A conversation
Not a chatbot→A presence
Not a post-test→A dialogue
The short version

A person-shaped presence trained on your approved content, voiced in your program's or practice's tone, available to everyone in every time zone. People who would have clicked past a slide deck or bounced off a contact form stay, talk, and show you exactly where their questions are. 03

Why medical education and healthcare teams are investing in avatars.

Learners and patients increasingly expect experiences that are interactive, personalized, and available on demand. Interactive avatars let CME providers and healthcare practices meet that expectation while generating the engagement and outcomes data your stakeholders ask for.

i

Scale your expertise

Extend the reach of your faculty, content, and care team across audiences, geographies, and time zones, without scaling headcount or front-desk load.

ii

Consistent, compliant messaging

Every interaction is grounded in approved content with closed-loop knowledge sources: safeguards that help prevent hallucinations, off-label discussion, and non-compliant responses.

iii

Personalized for everyone

Each learner or patient gets answers matched to their questions, interests, and level of understanding. Far more engaging than a static page or enduring material.

iv

Outcomes data you can report

Conversation analytics reveal knowledge gaps, emerging interests, and engagement trends in real time: evidence you can fold straight into outcomes reporting for supporters.

v

Reinforce live activities

Extend congress symposia, launch education, and disease-state awareness with an avatar that keeps the conversation going long after the session ends.

vi

Any language, any time zone

Avatars auto-detect and respond in the learner's native language: Spanish, Mandarin, Arabic, and many more. No selector, no configuration. Just conversation.

04

Features & Functionality

Every Divigner avatar ships with the same conversational core, then dials in the persona, guardrails, and tone for the program or practice it serves.

i

Voice & text in one

Learners can speak or type. The avatar replies in either modality, with natural lip-sync and a program-matched voice profile.

ii

Trained on your content

You state the knowledge base and approved documentation. The avatar answers from your sources, not the open internet.

iii

Smart routing

Questions outside the avatar's scope are routed to your designee with full transcript context, personal medical questions included.

iv

Compliance built-in

Educational disclaimers, PHI guardrails, and refusal policies are wired in by default and reviewed for every regulated program.

v

Drop-in embed

One snippet on your activity page or microsite, hosted on your domain, with no plugin or rebuild required. Updates push instantly.

vi

Analytics & transcripts

Every conversation is logged, summarized, and tagged by intent, so you can see what learners actually ask, activity after activity.

And it's engineered for regulated environments
01

Controlled knowledge sources

The avatar answers only from approved content. Closed-loop knowledge sources prevent hallucinations and off-label conversation. By design, not by hope.

02

Medical-legal-regulatory review support

Persona scripts, knowledge bases, and guardrail policies are packaged so your MLR and compliance teams can review exactly what the avatar knows and how it behaves.

03

Escalation pathways

Out-of-scope inquiries (personal medical questions, adverse-event mentions, anything sensitive) are routed to your designated contact with full context.

04

HIPAA-conscious design

Educational disclaimers up front, PHI guardrails throughout, and conversation de-identification for educational assessment.

05

Conversation logging & analytics

Sessions, engagement, and conversation paths are logged and summarized monthly: the raw material for outcomes reporting and continuous optimization.
